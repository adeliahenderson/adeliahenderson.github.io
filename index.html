More about me...

I will graduate with a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism, including concentrated minors in Criminal Justice and Law & Public Policy, from Mercer University in Macon, Ga.

As a student at Mercer's Center for Collaborative Journalism, I've taken courses related to news writing, community enagagement, photo/video production, coding, investigative reporting and media law/ethics.

Currently, I serve as the copy editor for Mercer's university newspaper, The Cluster. I've also been published with The Macon Telegraph, GPB Macon, student-run publication EngageCCJ, The Mercer Cluster and the Jones County News.
