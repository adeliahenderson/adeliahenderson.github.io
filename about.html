About Me

Hi! My name is Adelia, and I will graduate in December 2020 with my master's degree in journalism and public relations from the University of Georgia. I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in journalism from Mercer University in 2019.

I'm passionate about bettering the world around me through social media and digital communications. My work experience spans from political campaigns and government agencies to universities and nonprofits. Information is knowledge, and knowledge is power. Whether it's through a longform article, press release, Twitter thread, or video, it's vital that information is spread in ways that are fast, meaningful, and creative. The internet and social media link all corners of the globe together, and I'm excited to harness that power to inform publics and affect change.
